Full Article

Paragraph 1
Many young Americans, called Gen Z, now love Japan more than any other country. In the past, young people loved to visit France or India, but now Japan is the most popular place to go. Today, traveling to Japan is a big dream for many young people who want to see a different world. They see Japan as a special mix of beautiful old culture and very new technology.

Paragraph 2
Japan's pop culture and food are very famous and attractive to young travelers. Many people grew up watching anime like "Naruto" or "Pokémon" and playing video games. Netflix reported that the number of people watching anime grew three times larger over five years. Food is also a very big reason for travel. For example, the famous chef Anthony Bourdain once said he would choose to eat only in Tokyo for the rest of his life. Because of this interest, travel bookings for young people have increased by 1,300% since 2019.

Paragraph 3
Young travelers also visit Japan because it feels very safe and orderly. Japan is ranked as the number one safest country for crime and for using public transport. For instance, the streets are very clean even though there are almost no trash cans in public. People carry their trash home because they feel it is their own responsibility to keep the city clean. Many young Americans like this civility because it feels polite and calm compared to their homes. They think Japan feels like a "future" city with fast trains and kind people.

Paragraph 4
In conclusion, Japan is now a "cultural superpower" that influences many people around the world. It is not just about toys like Tamagotchi; it is about a way of life that young people admire. Japan has replaced France as the country that defines high class and good taste for the new generation. For many Gen Z travelers, Japan is the perfect place to find peace and order in a busy world.
